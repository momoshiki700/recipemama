English Breakfast Recipe

Ingredient Amount
Eggs 2.00 pcs
Bacon 2.00 slices
Sausages 2.00 pcs
Baked beans 0.50 cup
Tomatoes 1.00 whole
Mushrooms 0.50 cup
Toast 2.00 slices

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook the eggs: Fry the eggs to your preference (sunny side up, over-easy, etc.).
  2. Prepare the bacon and sausages: Fry the bacon until crisp and cook the sausages until thoroughly done.
  3. Heat the beans: Warm the baked beans in a small saucepan.
  4. Sauté veggies: Lightly sauté the tomatoes (halved) and mushrooms.
  5. Toast: Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  6. Assemble: Arrange all components on a plate for a hearty serving.
  7. Enjoy: Savor your traditional English breakfast!
